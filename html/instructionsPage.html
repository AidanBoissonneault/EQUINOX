EQUINOX

Equinox is a 2 player card game where players alternate between a light and dark deck with shared discard piles. The first player to empty their hand wins.

COMPONENTS:

Light Deck (52 cards)
Dark Deck (52 cards)
Light discard pile
Dark discard pile
Light player hands (7 cards each)
Dark player hands (7 cards each)

SETUP:

Both players draw 7 light cards and 7 dark cards.
Both players start on the light side.
Draw one card from each deck for each discard pile

HOW TO PLAY:

TURN STRUCTURE:

Play cards on your current side.
If one of your played cards is an
Ace or Face card
your turn continues, and you flip your hands to begin playing on the opposite deck. (light -> dark, dark -> light)
Your opponent does not flip decks with you.
Once your turn is over, their turn begins.

On the light deck, you must match the suit or value of either deck.

DARK DECK RULES:

On the dark deck, your must match the sum of either card on top of the discard pile.
You may play as many cards as needed to do so.
Ace count as 1 or 11.
Face cards count as any value 1-10, even while on a discard pile.

WINNING THE GAME:

A player can win the game in two ways:

  • Run out of cards in both hands
  • Have the least amount of cards in hand when a draw pile required to play a turn is empty, and a player cannot play.

ADDITIONAL INFO:

  • Your first selected card is the card that will appear on top of the discard pile. This is indicated with a different colour in the game.
  • You can hover on the respected decks to view how many cards are left in each deck to be drawn.
  • When playing a card to flip the hand you are playing, the flip card does not need to be on top.
  • There are sort buttons at the bottom left for your own convenience.
